
1) The highest number of reported vaccines that caused adverse events is Moderna.
2) Those who needed hospitalizations after taking the vaccine stayed in hospitals for a maximum of 5 days.
3) More than three-fourths of the reported events were by females.
4) Those who suffered the most from adverse reactions were between 30 to 50 years.
5) The main symptoms were headache, pyrexia, chills, and fatigue.
6) 13.34% of those who reported adverse events were hospitalized, 4.03% were life threatened, 2.66% were disabled, and 5.94% died.
7) 69.08% of those who died already had a problematic medical history and 53.02% suffered from allergies.
